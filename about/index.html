ABOUT  THE  CAREER  DEER

The Career Deer is an employment network for sharing job listings. This project is currently being developed by Moe Alaouie, Deena Chraim, and Garrett Haines for Dr. Bishu Panja's Winter 2022 Software Engineering Project class at Eastern Michigan University.

Our goal is to provide work-seeking individuals with a dynamic, relevant feed of career postings and a unique job-hunting experience.

The Career Deer will offer features for users to filter postings by field, location, organization, position, and salary. Business owners, recruiters, and entrepreneurs will be able to share their company's information and opportunities with potential job candidates.
